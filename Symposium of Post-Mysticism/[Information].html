Summary of the book and a small foreword

Character profiles are interspersed among the so-called "symposium" - essentially a dialogue hosted by Marisa Kirisame featuring Kanako Yasaka, Byakuren Hijiri, and Miko Toyosatomimi. These longer interviews involve the three discussing various topics while Hieda no Akyuu records their discussions.

Various articles from the Bunbunmaru Newspaper and the Kakashi Spirit News are also included at the end of the book, and deal with characters that weren't given an official profile, as well as certain events and various character interactions.

I, Saint, have proof read every piece of text at the same time I was transcribing this fanbook into HTML documents. I hope all grammar and syntax errors have been fixed during this arduous process. Now then, please do enjoy this work of wonder, ZUN has provided for us.
